### Overall Impression
The interface has high functional value and strong alignment with the CrossFit/brutalist theme, but it is visually exhausting. Visual clutter, typography clashes (mixing 5 fonts), and nested layouts create high cognitive load.

### What's Working
1. **Perfect CrossFit Domain Language**: The tone, abbreviations, and features (Working Max, RPE, ACWR) fit the target audience.
2. **Layout Versatility**: The ability to toggle between Sidebar, Grilla (Grid), and Papiro (Scroll) layouts is incredibly useful for different viewport sizes and usage contexts.

### Priority Issues
1. **[P1] Exercise Previews Inside Sidebar Buttons**:
   - *Why it matters*: Nested lists inside the sidebar button list create massive visual noise, lead to text truncation, and clutter the primary navigation area.
   - *Fix*: Remove exercise list previews from sidebar buttons. Keep them simple with title and count.
   - *Suggested command*: `/impeccable layout`
2. **[P1] Viewport Crowding & Sticky Header Stack on Mobile**:
   - *Why it matters*: Stacking the NavigationHeader, athlete header, ActiveDayHeader, and variation tabContainer leaves almost zero vertical space to view actual exercises on mobile.
   - *Fix*: Consolidate top headers and auto-hide/collapse the day selector on scroll.
   - *Suggested command*: `/impeccable adapt`
3. **[P2] Logo Image Hover Animation**:
   - *Why it matters*: Animating the logo on hover (`hover:scale-110` and electric-blue shadow) is a common AI design tell that adds no real value.
   - *Fix*: Remove the hover scale and filter drop-shadow from the logo `<img>` tag.
   - *Suggested command*: `/impeccable polish`
4. **[P2] Typographical Hierarchy & Clashing**:
   - *Why it matters*: Using 5 distinct font families (Anton, Inter, Mono, Caveat, Roboto Condensed) in close proximity creates visual confusion.
   - *Fix*: Limit Anton to big values and section titles, JetBrains Mono to data/mono labels, and Inter to body copy. Limit Caveat strictly to coach notes.
   - *Suggested command*: `/impeccable typeset`
5. **[P2] Card Side-Stripe Accent Borders**:
   - *Why it matters*: Using thick left borders (`border-l-[7px]`) on card headers violates the design system's absolute ban on side-stripes and feels visually aggressive.
   - *Fix*: Replace left borders with a full border or solid header background.
   - *Suggested command*: `/impeccable layout`
